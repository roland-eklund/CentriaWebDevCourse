Coding can be tough — bugs, errors, late nights, and too much coffee. But sometimes a good quote (or meme) is all you need to stay motivated and keep laughing through the chaos.

Heres three inspirational quotes

  • “Talk is cheap. Show me the code.” — Linus Torvalds, creator of Linux
  • “Programs must be written for people to read, and only incidentally for machines to execute.” — Harold Abelson, Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs
  • “First, solve the problem. Then, write the code.” — John Johnson
